## What this tool does

tutti-cli makes HTTP requests to the publicly accessible tutti.ch website and API endpoints to retrieve search results, in the same way a web browser would. No authentication credentials are bypassed, no private data is accessed, and no rate limits are deliberately circumvented.

## Data usage

- All data displayed by this tool originates from tutti.ch and belongs to its respective rights holders.
- Listing content (titles, descriptions, prices, images) is the property of the respective sellers and tutti.ch.
- Do not use this tool to scrape, republish, or systematically copy listing data.
- The local SQLite database (`~/.tutti/db.sqlite`) stores your personal search history on your own device only.
